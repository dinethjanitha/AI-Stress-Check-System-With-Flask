Deep Belly Breathing

Take a moment to relax and practice deep belly breathing to reduce stress and promote relaxation.

Instructions

  1. Find a comfortable seated position.
  2. Place one hand on your chest and the other hand on your belly.
  3. Inhale deeply through your nose, allowing your belly to rise and fill with air.
  4. Exhale slowly through your mouth, allowing your belly to fall and release the air.
  5. Continue breathing deeply, focusing on the rise and fall of your belly.
  6. Repeat for several minutes, allowing yourself to relax and unwind.
